Where the NightOwl-Hawk Wins

One vendor, one system

Hardware, dashboard, reporting, and support in a single package. Not a sensor you wire into someone else's platform, and not a set of instruments pieced together.

Built for GCD reporting

Pick the wells, the format, the recipient, and the schedule. The report sends itself, so getting data to the state stops being a monthly chore.

Try it before grant funds move

A 60-day loaner on a live well at zero cost. See the data on your own site before a dollar of grant money is committed.

Purpose-built for groundwater

Designed for wells and districts from the start, not adapted from industrial or oil-and-gas telemetry, and priced for the field.
