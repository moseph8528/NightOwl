NightOwl-Hawk

Cellular IoT Data Logger and Sensor Hub

The Hawk is a robust, plug-and-play IoT data logger and sensor hub designed to integrate with an extensive range of sensors and monitoring applications. Available in multiple connectivity, power, and housing variations, the Hawk gives you the flexibility to build exactly the remote monitoring solution you need.

Overview

The Hawk is purpose-built for remote monitoring deployments where flexibility and reliability matter. Whether you need to monitor water systems, environmental sensors, or equipment-level metrics, the Hawk adapts to your requirements.

Key advantages:

  • Modular I/O Architecture: Plug-in interface cards let you choose exactly which sensor protocols you need
  • Multiple Power Options: LiPo battery, external power, solar integration, or dual D Cell batteries
  • Secure Connectivity: LTE-M and NB-IoT cellular, with IoT Satellite version planned
  • Remote Device Management: Configure, update, and troubleshoot devices over the air
  • Enterprise-Grade Security: Military-level AES-256 encryption end-to-end
  • Field-Ready Housing: IP68 waterproof, IK08 impact-rated, GORE vents for pressure equalization

Power Options

The Hawk supports three distinct power configurations, allowing you to match your deployment environment and maintenance budget.

Rechargeable LiPo Battery

  • 3500mAh internal LiPo
  • Supports full-season deployments
  • Onboard charger with selectable charge rates
  • Optional external power (6-28V) or solar integration

D Cell LTC Battery Pack

  • 2 x D Cell Lithium Thionyl Chloride (LTC) batteries
  • Completely self-powered, no external charging
  • Extended field life for remote deployments
  • User-replaceable batteries

External Power

  • Input range: 6-28V DC (12V+ recommended for high load)
  • Integrated solar panel support
  • Continuous operation capability
  • Self-resetting fuse for protection

Input/Output Interface

The Hawk's sensor interfaces are managed by plug-in I/O cards, giving you unlimited flexibility to add the protocols and sensor types your application requires. Nine total I/O slots support a wide range of standard communication protocols and power options.

Onboard Digital Interface

Feature Detail
Digital Input 1x with configurable pull-up/pull-down; 0-40V DC range; pulse counting to 40MHz
Thresholds ON > 2V, OFF < 1V

Available I/O Cards

Choose one or more cards to build your sensor stack:

AgTech1 Card

1x Digital Input, 1x 4-20mA, SDI-12, I2C, 1-Wire/iButton, switched power (3.3V, 5V, 12V)

AgTech2 Card

4x Analog Inputs (0-30V), SDI-12, 1-Wire, switched power (3.3V, 5V, 12V)

RS-1 Card

RS485, 1x Analog Input (0-30V), 1x 4-20mA, 1-Wire, switched power

Serial Card

RS-232 TX/RX, TTL TX/RX, 2x Digital Inputs, switched power

Digital Card

2x Analog Inputs (0-30V), 5x Digital Inputs, max 40Hz pulse counting

Bluetooth+ Card

Full I/O plus integrated Bluetooth module (BGM240PA22VNA3)

Housing Options

The Hawk is available in two rugged housing configurations, both IP68 waterproof and IK08 impact-rated, with GORE vents for pressure equalization. Non-branded enclosures support optional white labeling.

HawkLipo Housing

  • Dimensions: 185 x 150 x 30 mm (7.3 x 5.9 x 1.2")
  • Houses PCB, I/O Card, and 3500mAh LiPo battery
  • IP68 waterproof, IK08 impact-rated
  • GORE vent for pressure equalization
  • Multiple mounting options (screws, bolts, cable ties, rivets)
  • 2 cable glands for waterproof cable entry

Hawk2D Housing

  • Dimensions: 180 x 119 x 39 mm (7.1 x 4.7 x 1.5")
  • Houses PCB, I/O Card, and 2 x D Cell LTC batteries
  • IP68 waterproof, IK08 impact-rated
  • GORE vent for pressure equalization
  • Multiple mounting options (screws, bolts, cable ties, rivets)
  • 2 cable glands for waterproof cable entry
