Input/Output Architecture

Accepts one plug-in I/O card providing up to nine card-defined inputs and outputs, depending on the selected card. Sensor-power outputs and supported voltages depend on the selected I/O card.

Available I/O Cards:
AgTech1

1x Digital In, 1x 4-20mA, SDI-12, I2C, 1-Wire/iButton, switched-ground out, 3.3/5/12V sensor power

AgTech2

4x Analog In (0-30V), SDI-12, 1-Wire, switched-ground out, 3.3/5/12V sensor power

RS-1

RS485, 1x Analog In, 1x Digital In, 1x 4-20mA, 1-Wire, switched-ground out, 3.3/5/12V sensor power

Serial

RS-232 TX/RX, TTL TX/RX, 1x Analog In, 2x Digital In, switched-ground out, 5/12V sensor power

Digital

2x Analog In, 5x Digital In, pulse counting, switched-ground out, 5/12V sensor power

Bluetooth+

Bluetooth BGM240PA22VNA3, 1x Analog In, 1x Digital/Pulse In, switched-ground out, SDI-12, I2C, 1x 4-20mA, 3.3/5/12V sensor power
