Cellular IoT Data Logger and Sensor Hub
The Hawk is a robust, plug-and-play IoT data logger and sensor hub designed to integrate with an extensive range of sensors and monitoring applications. Available in multiple connectivity, power, and housing variations, the Hawk gives you the flexibility to build exactly the remote monitoring solution you need.

Overview

The Hawk is purpose-built for remote monitoring deployments where flexibility and reliability matter. Whether you need to monitor water systems, environmental sensors, or equipment-level metrics, the Hawk adapts to your requirements.

Key Advantages:
  • Modular I/O Architecture: Plug-in interface cards let you choose exactly which sensor protocols you need
  • Multiple Power Options: LiPo battery, external power, solar integration, or dual D Cell batteries
  • Secure Connectivity: LTE-M and NB-IoT cellular, with IoT Satellite version planned
  • Remote Device Management: Configure, update, and troubleshoot devices over the air
