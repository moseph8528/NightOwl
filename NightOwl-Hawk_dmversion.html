Cellular IoT Data Logger and Sensor Hub

The Hawk is a robust, plug-and-play IoT data logger and sensor hub designed to integrate with an extensive range of sensors and monitoring applications. Available in multiple connectivity, power, and housing variations, the Hawk gives you the flexibility to build exactly the remote monitoring solution you need.

Connectivity LTE-M / NB-IoT + GPS
I/O Capacity 9 Configurable Slots
Power Options LiPo, D Cell, External
Security AES-256 Encryption
Why the Hawk

Built for the field. The Hawk combines enterprise-grade reliability with modular flexibility, giving you exactly what you need without bloat.

I/O Interface & Housing

Input/Output Architecture

Nine configurable I/O slots with plug-in cards. All cards support flexible power delivery (3.3V, 5V, 12V).

Available I/O Cards:
AgTech1

1x Digital Input, 1x 4-20mA, SDI-12, I2C, 1-Wire/iButton

AgTech2

4x Analog Inputs (0-30V), SDI-12, 1-Wire

RS-1

RS485, 1x Analog Input, 1x 4-20mA, 1-Wire

Serial

RS-232 TX/RX, TTL TX/RX, 2x Digital Inputs

Digital

2x Analog Inputs, 5x Digital Inputs, pulse counting

Bluetooth+

Full I/O + Bluetooth module (BGM240PA22VNA3)

Housing Options
HawkLipo 185 x 150 x 30 mm. PCB + I/O + 3500mAh LiPo. IP68/IK08. Multiple mounting options.
Hawk2D 180 x 119 x 39 mm. PCB + I/O + 2x D Cell. IP68/IK08. Multiple mounting options.
