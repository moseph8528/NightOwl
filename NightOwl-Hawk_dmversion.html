I/O Architecture and Housing

Plug-in I/O Cards

The Hawk's modular I/O design uses standardized cards that define the device's sensor capabilities. Select one or more cards to build your monitoring stack. All cards support flexible power delivery (3.3V, 5V, 12V switched outputs).

AgTech1

1x Digital Input, 1x 4-20mA, SDI-12, I2C, 1-Wire/iButton

AgTech2

4x Analog Inputs (0-30V), SDI-12, 1-Wire

RS-1

RS485, 1x Analog Input, 1x 4-20mA, 1-Wire

Serial

RS-232 TX/RX, TTL TX/RX, 2x Digital Inputs

Digital

2x Analog Inputs, 5x Digital Inputs, pulse counting

Bluetooth+

Full I/O + integrated Bluetooth module

Task Management and Integration

Powerful onboard task management allows the Hawk to respond to sensor thresholds and events independently. Even when cellular coverage is lost, the device stores data and processes local tasks, forwarding everything when connectivity resumes.
